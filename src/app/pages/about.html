Passion for Adventure, Purpose in Travel

Many of us feel our money should be spent on experiences rather than things. Experiences enrich our lives, open our minds, and create wonderful, lasting memories. Route on Wheels is a community dedicated to exploring, learning & growing.

It gives an opportunity to discover simple ways to lead an extraordinary life – one that is truly meaningful to you. We have the knowledge, we are connected, and we are absolutely passionate about designing the best holiday for you – enriching your life with amazing experiences.

We offer travel experiences from the Mighty Himalayas in the North to the Palm-fringed beaches of the South, from Ethnic Tribes of the North East to the colourful Thar Desert of Western India.

Our Philosophy

"Route on Wheels" not only refers to the trips but also symbolizes a deeper connection— A journey that's symbolised both physical and emotional, bringing people back to their roots through travel, rediscovering traditions, cultural heritage, history, and personal connections with ancestral places.

Route and Wheels Symbolised

In ancient times, the routes have played a crucial role in shaping great civilizations, the complex societies with well-developed social organizations, culture, and way of life. These ancient pathways facilitated the exchange of goods, ideas, technologies, and cultures, contributing to the rise of powerful empires and the development of global economies.

Walking along trade routes was one of the best ways to connect with different traditions, cultures, and economies. The wheel is one of the most significant inventions connecting civilizations, powering economies, and shaping history.
